## Specifications:

- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Dimensions: 400px Ã— 200px (or similar 2:1 aspect ratio)
- Style: Official university logos
- Note: Logos will be automatically converted to grayscale and shown with reduced opacity for a professional look

## Where to Find Logos:

Most universities provide official logo downloads on their brand/identity pages:
- Search for "[University Name] brand guidelines" or "[University Name] logo download"
- Use high-resolution PNG versions
- Ensure you have the right to use these logos

## Alternative:

If you don't have access to logos, you can use text-based placeholders by keeping the fallback behavior in the component.
